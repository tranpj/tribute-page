Stack Overflow History

Created by Jeff Atwood and Joel Spolsky

Brief History

  • April 2008 Name of website choosen by readers of Jeff Atwood's programming blog Coding Horror
  • July 2008 Invitations set out by Jeff Atwood to his subscribers to take part in the private beta of the new website
  • September 2008 Public beta announced. Generally public now able to use the website
  • May 2010 $6 million in venture capital raised

Empowering the world to develop technology through collective knowledge
